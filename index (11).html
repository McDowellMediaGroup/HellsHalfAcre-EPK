About

The Bloody Third Ward

Hells Half Acre is a heavy metal band from Fort Worth, Texas — named after the city’s most notorious red-light district, a lawless stretch of saloons, outlaws, and vice that put Fort Worth on the map in the 1800s. The name fits. The music delivers on every promise the name makes.

Built around the vocal ferocity of Josh Stone McDade, the guitar weaponry of Ryan Shultz, the thunderous low end of Noah Niemann, and the relentless percussion of Christian Bednarski — HHA brings heavy, aggressive metal to stages across North Texas with zero compromise and maximum damage.

They are new. They are hungry. And they are already turning heads — opening for Cody Parks & The Dirty South on the Steel Panther tour and building one of the most rabid fanbases in the DFW underground. The Bloody Third Ward is just getting warm.
